⚠ What Journalists Are Missing

  • The Acosta Contradiction: Under oath, Acosta denied the "belonged to intelligence" claim he reportedly made to the Trump transition team. No major outlet has reconciled these two statements side-by-side with source documents.
  • The Wexner Proffer (EFTA02731082): This 86-page document effectively explains the origin of ALL of Epstein's wealth as theft from Wexner -- yet coverage treats Epstein's fortune as mysterious.

  • Dalton School Records Missing: Records from Epstein's time teaching at Dalton were subpoenaed but "they do not have the records." No investigation into why these records vanished.
  • The Video Evidence Chain: Hidden cameras, video disks extracted by Palm Beach Sheriff's Computer Crime Unit, VHS surveillance tapes -- the full evidentiary chain of recorded material has not been mapped.

5 The Wexner Financial Pipeline (The Smoking Gun)

EFTA02731082: Origin of ALL Epstein WealthCritical
The Wexner proffer is arguably the most important single document in the entire release. It reveals:
  • Epstein stole "several hundred million dollars" from Les Wexner
  • This theft + fees "appears to account for VIRTUALLY ALL of Epstein's wealth"
  • Epstein obtained power of attorney in 1991 (the year Robert Maxwell died)
  • Controlled ALL Wexner personal finances with "virtually no oversight"
  • Epstein falsely told Wexner he had other clients
This single document demolishes the "mystery billionaire" narrative. Epstein was a thief who exploited one man's trust.

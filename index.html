About This Project

This is an automated analysis of publicly released documents from the Jeffrey Epstein case. Every document published by the Department of Justice, FBI, and federal courts has been downloaded, converted to text, and searched against 67 tracked terms covering named individuals, institutions, and key concepts.

Each document that produces matches is classified by severity on a 1-10 scale based on its content: direct evidence of crimes against minors (10), law enforcement records (8-9), financial records (6-7), and administrative materials (1-5). All findings link back to the original source PDFs hosted on archive.org.

All content is extracted from publicly released government documents. No editorial content has been added. This is an open-source research project.

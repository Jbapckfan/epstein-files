05 Key Takeaway

The documents reveal that Trump (339 red flags), Prince Andrew (208), Dershowitz (168), Acosta (158), and Brunel (142) have the highest concentration of serious allegations in Epstein-related context. Trump alone appears in 96 findings mentioning minors, 43 trafficking references, 36 sexual assault contexts, and 46 murder/threat documents. By contrast, Obama (3 total red flags) and Biden (0) have essentially zero presence in any serious allegation category.
